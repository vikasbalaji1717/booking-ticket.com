Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about booking or cancellations? We have answers to help make your booking easier.

How do I cancel my booking?
You can cancel your ticket by logging into your account, navigating to the "My Bookings" page, and clicking the "Cancel" button next to your active booking. Note that cancellations are only allowed before the departure day.
Is it mandatory to carry a physical copy of my e-ticket?
No, a physical printout is not mandatory. You can show the PDF ticket downloaded onto your phone or present the PNR booking SMS along with a valid photo ID (Aadhar Card, Driving License, etc.) during boarding.
How are refunds processed for cancelled tickets?
Once you click cancel on your dashboard, the system initiates the refund simulation instantly. The refund status in your transaction logs changes to "Refunded", and in a production scenario, the amount is credited back to your original payment source.
